About Us



I have started making handmade products as a hobby. I made different varieties of floral jewellery, hair accessories, embroidery hoops as per customers requirements. But as the time passes, I feel like there are lots of people who wants customised products/gifts for their loved ones. So, I started spreading my small business and got a good response from clients. I started online/offline courses for those who were interested to learn new things and got a wonderful response from them. After few months of being live, the support and love that I got from my viewers/customers/followers was just overwhelming.


My effort is always to give good quality handmade product, budget combos, without stressing on your wallet!
